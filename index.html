Jubilee Hills House Building Cooperative Society which was formed in the year 1963 has about 5000 members and about 3325 plots and is a prestigious place to live in. It is an affluent suburban neighbourhood in western part of Hyderabad, Telangana and is sandwiched between the wealthy commercial area of Banjara Hills and is located within 2 KMS of Hyderabad IT hub of Hitech City to its southeast is the Kasu Brahmananda Reddy National Park, which is one of the largest urban National Parks in India. It is stated that JH Society is one of the largest residential colony in Asia and known for its vastness and endless boundaries.

The society also has reputed institutions in various sectors; Hospitals, Educational Institutions, Temples, etc.
